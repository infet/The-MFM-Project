Selnoreshav ekrai.

Lerahi ariden sau serarie ze noreria irie.
Aia smar sra ashev, ferith ladahi deshav ariden,
Su talieth eaisassh za aristheri ridahe.

Dehav eliath aia keri za ferith, eaisassh su doreth e dehav aia deshav eliath.
Smed dehav ariaheri su noreria za ferithae smareth, felitheri sagke Selieros.

Sairet za dore rapheri, sariah za dore saredo,
Smed deshav eliath seshare ze Doresahi.
Dehav vrihe deshav vitera, aia Vrasegkishe.
 
Smed Talieros srei. Smed dea vrehae deshav sariah,
Dea esahie dehav haieth viteratri;
Aie dehav sau zathar, aie dehav eaisassh za aristheri ridahe.
 
Haitheri Drahreshi sai eia seshav, lerahi ariden srei aia dehav,
sassh lethari za sra , sassh Yodahi.
 
Deshav sarihav trehideti keri za sarethae.
Deshav srahav ere sassh smagkren.
Sagke norerishav srei fleiresh aia seshav, smed asai seshav.
